Department Achievements:

1.In the District Student-Youth Science Fair 2013 which was organised by Youth Services Department, Government of West Bengal in collaboration with School Education Department, Higher Education Department, W.B. State Council of Science & Technology, Govt. of West Bengal & Birla Industrial and Technological Museum, two groups containing total of four students who had participated were awarded the 1st and the 3rd position respectively at the District level. Both the groups had presented working models on Utilisation of Wind Energy & Solar Energy. The students who secured the 1st position got an opportunity to participate at the final at Kolkata (West Bengal) for state level competition.

2.In the two days Workshop on Robotics three groups of students from the Department of Electrical Engineering had secured the 1st, 2nd & 3rd position respectively. They got an opportunity to participate in the Grand Finale of ROBOZEST, 2014 which was held at IIT-Bombay (Mumbai, Maharashtra) where they had to compete with around 250 Groups from different Colleges all around India.

 

Event Organization:

1. Electrical Engineering Department organised ROBOZEST, 2014 a two days Workshop on ROBOTICS at our college campus on 23rd & 24th February, 2014.It was a National Level Robotics Championship which was conducted by RoboSpecies Private Limited in Association with Aakaar14, IIT-Bombay & Accredited by International Accreditation Organization, Houston, U.S.A. In that event a total of 106 students participated.

2. Electrical Engineering department organised a one day Seminar on PLC at the Institute.
