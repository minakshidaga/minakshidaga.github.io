From The Director's Desk


Dr.B.P Sarkar,
M.TECH(IIT Delhi)PHD (Manchester),
FIE,C(ENG),FTA,NRDC AWARDEE. 

Considering the rapid growth of in field of industries the demand for engineers has increased rapidly.IMPS College of Engineering & Technology aims to provide best of education in West Bengal.The students are inspired to develop leadership quality.

To meet the technological demands,AICTE ,Ministry of Human Resource Development,Govt. of India,Department of Higher education,Govt of West Bengal, Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technologyhave supported us with their blessings to set up this college.

The College started with lots of hope,aspirations.It is mirror of excellence which makes it a difference.The college is located in Malda to add new dimension to north bengal.The institute provides graduation course in departments like Computer Science & Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Civil Engineering and humanities department.
