AntiRagging

 

Measures We Take

The following measures we take to avoid ragging in our college campus:-
a)An Anti-Ragging Committee has been made in our college as per norms of AICTE to ensure zero-ragging.
b)For fresher's seprate class-rooms and hostel is provided so, that we can save them from the seniors contact.
c)To create no ragging atmosphere in our college, an Anti-Ragging Board is there which clearly mention the punishment for the raggers.

 

Constitution Of Anti-Ragging Committee

According to the "West Bengal Prohibition of Ragging in Educational Institute Act 2000" the student who are involved in ragging should punish strictly and it has been made compulsory for colleges and other Educational Institutions to register F.I.R.s against them.

Punishment For Ragging

The following actions are taken against student for indulging & alerting ragging:-

1. Suspension from attending classes.
2. Suspension from hostel.
3. F.I.R. must be filed against raggers.
4. Fine could be taken from them.
5.Rustication from institution.
