Vision & Mission

Vision:

Impart quality education in Electronics and Communication Engineering through excellent teaching, keeping abreast of the changes in technology and environment..

Mission :

To provide strong foundation in Computer Science and Engineering, prepare students for professional career and higher education, and inculcate research interest.

To be abreast of the technological advances in a rapidly changing world.

To impart skills to come up with socially acceptable solutions to real world problems, upholding ethical values.

Introduction :

For the last few years, the department has been imparting a state-of the-art education and training in the field of computer science and allied areas and regularly producing efficient and outstanding professionals. The department offers B.Tech programme with excellent infrastructure and well qualified and experienced faculty.

Established in the year 2010, the Department of Computer Science and Engineering has made great strides during the short period of its existence. The faculty members of the department have been guiding research works and are representing different academic bodies like Board of Studies (Engineering UG & PG), Academic Council, Faculty of Engineering etc. The department has been taking initiative for the preparation and revision of scheme and syllabus for the B.Tech degree courses in Computer Science Engineering. Most of the students are getting placements and internship opportunities in reputed companies.

We are working with an objective to contribute our part to develop India as a Super Power in the area of Computer Science by imparting knowledge of an international standard to young Engineers and Computer Scientists.
