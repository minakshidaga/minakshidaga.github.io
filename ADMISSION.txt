                    <p>General Candidates must pass H.S.  Candidates must pass Higher Secondary (10+2) Examination of West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education or equivalent examination from a

                        90% of  the  total  seats  shall  be  filled  up from the West  Bengal Joint  Entrance  Examination  (JEM)  and 10%  from  All  India  Engineering Entrance  Examination  (AIEEE)
