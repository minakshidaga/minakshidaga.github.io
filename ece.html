Vision & Mission

Vision:

Impart quality education in Electronics and Communication Engineering through excellent teaching, keeping abreast of the changes in technology and environment..

Mission:

Offer UG programs at par with the curricula offered by institutions of national importance.

Provide robust academic and co – curricular ambience for moulding graduates to high quality professionals in practice and higher education.

Prepare graduates adaptable to the changing requirements of the society, through lifelong learning coupled with an ethical outlook and dedication.

Introduction:

The department has designed Basic Electronics, Analog Electronics, Digital Electronics, Signal and Systems, Circuit Theory, Solid State Device, Communication and Coding Theory, Measurement, Microprocessor laboratories to fulfill the academic needs of students. Each lab is designed according to the MAKAUT syllabi and provides enough scope to students to perform the experiments individually so that they can implement their theoretical perception in the practical circuits.

The department has well experienced faculty with postgraduate and doctoral qualifications from prestigious institutions with many international and national publications to their credit. The faculty members regularly were updating their skills by attending and organizing refresher courses in their areas of specializations.
