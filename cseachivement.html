Department Achievements

In the two days Workshop on Indias First Cloud Computing Championship(14th & 15th March-2015) three students from the Department of CSE and one student from ECE had secured the 1st, 2nd ,3rd & 4th position respectively. They got an opportunity to participate in the Grand Finale of Indias First Cloud Computing Championship- 2015 which was held at IIT-Bhubaneswar where they had to compete with around 250 Groups from different Colleges all around India.
