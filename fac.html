Other Facilities


Accomodation:-

IMPSCET has separate hostels for boys and girls. For 1st year,there is a separate hostel outside the campus. Transportation,Power-Supply,Net-Lab,Medical care and all other facilities are available in college campus.

Auditorium:-

A large auditorium is there is our college campus where all the cultural programmes are conducted.

Transportation:-

A college bus is provided for faculty and students to make their journey safe comfortable and economical.

Indoor and Outdoor Sports:-

A large playing field is there in college campus which supports variety of games i.e. football,cricket,volley-ball etc. The indoor sports include carams,table-tennis,chess etc.

Internet Browsing Centre:-

The college has Internet Centre contains 40 computers with 10 MBPS dedicated internet connectivity, and is open all year round.

Security and Safety:-

IMPSCET has a large team of professionally-trained security personnel patrolling and guarding the campus 24X7 to ensure safety of students, staff and infrastructure.

Power Service:-

The institute is equipped with uninterrupted Power Service facility which provides power for 24 X7 hrs, both in college and hostel.
