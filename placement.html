Introduction

The primary goal of placement cell at IMPSCET is to provide full career relate support to its students.Every student wishes to get placed in a top organization.The Placement Cell gives its best effort to bring in multiple companies from varied industries to match the expectations and aspirations of the students. The cell offers answers to all market related queries.

Besides the placement cell,a training cell is also maintained at IMPS.As the competition increases,training plays a vital role for placement.Thus the institution tries to equip its student with various life skills.At IMPSCET students are provide several training programes some of them which include soft skill development,Aptitude Test, Group Discussions, Personal Interviews, Grooming seasons etc.It also tries to arrange different workshops and seminars from top notch industries.

The main objectives of the training & placement cell incudes:

1. Developing deep technical knowledge of the students

2. Build confidence and Personality of students to face any challenges

3. To motivate and guide students for higher studies and provide them training for exams like CAT,GATE,GMAT,GRE,etc.
